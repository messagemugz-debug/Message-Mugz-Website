Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Message Mugz.

How do I upload my video?

After purchase, you’ll receive a secure link to upload your video message.

How long will the video be available?

Videos are securely stored for 12 months from the date of upload.

Can I order multiple mugs?

Yes, each order gets a unique QR code linked to your video.

Is the QR code reusable?

No, each QR code links to a unique video for the intended recipient.
