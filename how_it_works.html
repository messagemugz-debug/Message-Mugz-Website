How It Works

Your personalised video message, delivered in the most memorable way.

1. Choose Your Mug

Select from our Occasion, Birthday, Father’s Day, Mother’s Day or Love collections.

2. Upload Your Video Message

After checkout, you’ll receive a link to upload your video message.

3. We Generate Your QR Code

We securely host your video and create a unique QR code linked to your message.

4. The Mug Is Printed & Delivered

Your personalised mug is printed and shipped straight to the recipient.

5. They Scan & Enjoy the Message

The recipient scans the QR code and watches your message—making your gift unforgettable.
